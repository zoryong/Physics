⚛️ AN'S PHYSICS

Physics Simulator

A collection of interactive simulators built to support the teaching and learning of physics. Adjust the sliders to change the conditions and see how each phenomenon responds, making abstract concepts easier to grasp and explore in the classroom or at home.

Currently 10 simulators are live. If you find a bug or have an idea for improvement, please email me and I'll take a look.
